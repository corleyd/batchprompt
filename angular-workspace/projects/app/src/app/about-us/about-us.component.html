About Us

BatchPrompt helps businesses and data professionals work with AI models more effectively. Our platform connects large language models with practical data processing workflows.

We started BatchPrompt because we noticed that while AI models are powerful, using them at scale often requires significant technical expertise and infrastructure. We built a platform that aims to make batch AI processing more accessible by providing a simple interface for uploading and processing data.

Our diverse team brings together expertise in artificial intelligence, data engineering, user experience design, and enterprise software development. We understand the challenges faced by data analysts, researchers, content creators, and business professionals who need to process large volumes of data efficiently. Whether you're generating product descriptions for thousands of items, analyzing customer feedback at scale, or extracting insights from unstructured text, BatchPrompt provides the tools to get the job done.

We believe that powerful AI capabilities should be accessible to everyone, not just those with extensive technical resources. That's why we've built BatchPrompt to be intuitive, reliable, and scalable, allowing users to focus on their data and insights rather than the complexity of AI infrastructure. As we continue to grow and evolve, we remain committed to empowering our users with cutting-edge AI technology that drives real business value.
